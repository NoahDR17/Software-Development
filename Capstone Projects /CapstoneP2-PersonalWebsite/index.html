About Me

I've always been passionate about the the culinary arts, appreciating the creative aspects inherent in the craft of cooking. The fullfilling and ever-evolving atmosphere of professional kitchens held particular interest to me. Initially, I charted my course toward a career as a chef, but my journey in the industry prompted a significant realization. I discovered a growing interest in the realm of technology, with software development, in particular, captivating my attention due to its increasing demand. This epiphany led me to make a deliberate shift in my focus towards Full-Stack Software Development. My pursuit of this field included dedicated self-study, wherein I was instantly enthralled by the intellectual challenge of leveraging code to unravel intricate problems. By June 2023, I had graduated from college, and since then, I have steadfastly pursued my newfound passion for software development. My efforts encompassed enrolling in online courses, participating in professional meetups, and undertaking personal projects, one of which being this website. At present, I am eager to channel my acquired skills and knowledge into a professional environment. I believe that my unique blend of hospitality and technical expertise would provide great value to anyone i work with.
